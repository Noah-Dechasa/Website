About Me

Hi, I'm Noah

I’m from Addis Ababa, the heart of Ethiopia, a country known for its ancient history and rich culture.

From an early age, I was surrounded by the sounds, flavors, and vibrance of Ethiopian life, and these experiences shaped who I am today. Ethiopia has such a unique place in the world, and I’m proud to carry that heritage wherever I go.

Why I Love Ethiopia

Ethiopia is not just my birthplace; it's where my roots run deep. The sense of community, the pride in our history, and the love for our food and culture have all influenced the way I live today. I’m proud of where I’m from and the incredible diversity of this beautiful country.
